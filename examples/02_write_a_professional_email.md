# Example 02 — Write a professional email

## Prompt
Write a professional email asking to reschedule an interview to next week.

## Candidate Response A
Subject: Interview Reschedule Request

Hi [Name],
Thank you for the opportunity to interview for the [Role] position. I’m excited to speak with you.
Would it be possible to reschedule our interview to sometime next week? I had an unexpected conflict arise.
I apologize for the inconvenience and appreciate your flexibility. Please let me know what times work best.

Best regards,
[Your Name]

## Candidate Response B
Hey, I can’t make it. Can we do next week instead? Thanks.
