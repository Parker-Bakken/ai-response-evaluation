# Example 06 — Explain photosynthesis simply

## Prompt
Explain photosynthesis in simple terms.

## Candidate Response A
Photosynthesis is how plants make their own food. They use sunlight, water, and carbon dioxide from the air to create sugar, which gives them energy to grow. Oxygen is released as a byproduct, which is why plants help produce the air we breathe.

## Candidate Response B
Photosynthesis is a photochemical process involving chlorophyll pigments and electron transport chains that generate ATP and NADPH during the light-dependent reactions.
