# Example 03 — Recommend a budget laptop

## Prompt
Recommend a budget laptop for a college student under $600. Needs good battery and light weight.

## Candidate Response A
A good option is a 13–14" laptop with at least 8GB RAM and a modern i5/Ryzen 5 CPU.
Look for 10+ hour battery ratings and under ~3.2 lbs. Examples: Acer Swift series, ASUS ZenBook entry models, or Lenovo IdeaPad Slim lines.
Avoid 4GB RAM models. If possible, choose 512GB SSD.

## Candidate Response B
Buy the newest MacBook Pro.
