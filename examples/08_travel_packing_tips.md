# Example 08 — Travel packing tips

## Prompt
What should I pack for a 3-day trip?

## Candidate Response A
For a 3-day trip, pack essentials such as:
- 3 outfits
- toiletries
- phone charger
- travel documents
- comfortable shoes
- weather-appropriate jacket

If flying, keep liquids under airline limits and carry valuables in your personal bag.

## Candidate Response B
Bring whatever you want. Every trip is different.
